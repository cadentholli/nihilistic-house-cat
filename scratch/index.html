Top Midwest Emo Bands (imho)

  1. Free Throw
    • Perfect blend of mathy riffs and melodic breakdowns
    • Nashville-based emo revival band
    • They have a song titled: "Hey Ken, Someone Methodically Mushed the Donuts"
  2. Mom Jeans.
    • Twinkly guitar with interesting song structures and timing changes
    • Bay Area band known for midwest emo revival style
    • Sounds like your buddy's band that is awful but you support him anyway cause you are a good friend
  3. Tigers Jaw
    • Scranton-based band blending emo and indie rock
    • Signature dual vocals from Ben and Brianna
    • "Plane Vs. Tank Vs. Submarine" is a masterpiece
